Join us on our Journey

Welcome to Pure Heart Yoga, where the journey of self-discovery and holistic wellness begins. We are James and Gia, a couple united by our shared passion for yoga, and we invite you to join us in building a vibrant and supportive Yoga Community. With love, dedication, and a commitment to spreading the transformative power of yoga, we have embarked on this beautiful path together. Our story began five years ago when Gia walked into James' yoga class as a curious beginner. Little did we know that this encounter would mark the beginning of an extraordinary journey that would forever change our lives. Gia's genuine interest in the practice and her unwavering commitment to growth and self-improvement inspired James to nurture her budding potential. As Gia continued attending James' classes regularly, her dedication and enthusiasm for yoga blossomed. She soon realized that yoga was not just a physical exercise but a profound philosophy and way of life. Fuelled by her deepening passion, Gia decided to pursue formal training as a yoga instructor, eager to share the countless benefits she had experienced firsthand. Our journey took an unexpected turn three years ago when we made the bold decision to move to central New York amidst the unprecedented challenges posed by the global pandemic. Instead of allowing fear and uncertainty to deter us, we chose to embrace this transition as an opportunity to establish Pure Heart Yoga and bring our shared vision to life. We firmly believed that even in times of crisis, the power of yoga could help heal and uplift individuals and communities. Pure Heart Yoga became our sanctuary, our haven of peace and harmony in a chaotic world. The name itself holds deep significance for us. It represents the essence of our intentions: to create a space where yoga is practiced not just for the physical benefits, but as a gateway to nurturing the purity and authenticity of the heart. Our heartfelt desire is to guide others toward finding balance, self-acceptance, and inner tranquility through the practice of yoga. At Pure Heart Yoga, we strive to cultivate a sense of belonging and community. We believe that yoga is not just an individual practice but a collective experience that can create lasting bonds and connections. Our vision extends beyond the studio walls as we aspire to build a strong and supportive Yoga Community that embraces diversity and fosters personal growth. Through our classes, workshops, and retreats, we aim to provide a nurturing space where individuals can explore the many facets of yoga, deepen their practice, and develop a profound understanding of themselves. We understand that each individual's journey is unique, and we tailor our offerings to meet the diverse needs and aspirations of our students. Yoga has the power to transform lives, and we consider it a privilege to be able to share this gift with others. We invite you to join us on this transformative journey of self-discovery, healing, and personal growth. Whether you are a seasoned yogi or taking your first steps on the mat, Pure Heart Yoga welcomes you with open arms. Together, let us create a vibrant and thriving Yoga Community where love, acceptance, and support flow abundantly. Let us share the beauty of yoga, not only as a physical practice but as a way of life. Pure Heart Yoga
